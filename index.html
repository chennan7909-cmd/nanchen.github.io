Transforming complex data into clear, actionable insights.

I am an MBAI student at Ontario Tech University, currently pursuing my studies in Data Visualization and Storytelling. My work focuses on creating meaningful visual experiences that make complex data accessible, engaging, and grounded in real-world context.

With a foundation in both technical analysis and design principles, I create visualizations that not only inform but also inspire action. My recent projects explore emerging technologies, financial markets, and human-centered phenomena through the lens of data — blending analytical rigor with thoughtful visual communication.
